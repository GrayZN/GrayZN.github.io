# Songs For Christmas Vinyl

We finally got it together and are releasing my first “Songs for Christmas” box-set on vinyl on November 9, 2018. It includes the first five EPs, from 2001-2006: Noel, Hark, Ding-Dong, Joy, and Peace. Pre-order is now available on AKR. Sorry it took so long. As you can see, we’re making an effort to get all the old stuff on vinyl before I release any new stuff. The new box-set includes five LPs, original artwork by me and Stephen Halker, the family Christmas portrait, as well as all lyrics and chord charts. You can still get the stickers and all the other stuff in the CD box-set (essays, comics, etc.), but we had to cut them here to keep costs down. Hope you enjoy! Every day is Christmas when I’m with you.

# The Avalanche Vinyl

Hi. The Avalanche is finally available on vinyl (release date August 31, 2018). You can pre-order through AKR here. This was an album of outtakes and extras from the Illinois album (originally released in 2006). It’s a lot of music in here that can sometimes feel like you’re chewing on neon Play-Doh, but just remember the “single body structure really improves cargo flexibility,” according to Consumer Reports. Better late than never! Peace to you. XOXO
